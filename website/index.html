Features

Built for daily photo tracking

PocketPic keeps the workflow simple. Capture, review, and export — all on macOS. Your photos stay private in your Photos library.

📷

Menu bar capture

Quick photo access from the menu bar. Open the full app for browsing your library and managing streaks.

👻

Alignment overlay

Your previous photo ghosts over the viewfinder with adjustable opacity for perfect framing consistency.

🎬

Export video

Turn your series into H.264 or HEVC video. Choose frame rate and resolution. Save to Photos or Share.

👁

Eye alignment

Optional Vision-based face alignment keeps your face centered and consistent across all frames.

📅

Photo library

Browse photos by month. Track current and longest streaks. Full-screen detail view.

🔒

Privacy first

Hide thumbnails in the app gallery. Your photos stay in Photos app, completely untouched.

How it works

Three steps to your time-lapse

Capture daily

Open the camera, line up with yesterday's overlay, and save one photo to your PocketPic album.

Build your streak

Review your library, track streaks, and select or delete photos as your project grows.

Export video

Generate a montage with optional eye alignment. Share your transformation anywhere.

macOS

Native, notarized, and built with SwiftUI

Uses AVFoundation for capture, PhotoKit for library sync, and Vision for face detection—all on-device and secure.
