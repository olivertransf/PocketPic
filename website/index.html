PocketPic

One photo a day.
Watch yourself change.

A daily photo journal for macOS — ghost overlays for consistent framing, then export a time-lapse of your streak.

Download for macOS

macOS 15+ · Free · Runs on-device

What it does

Built for a daily photo practice

Shoot from the menu bar, line up with yesterday, and keep everything in your Photos library. No accounts. No cloud uploads.

  • Menu bar capture

    Snap your daily frame from the menu bar without opening the full window. The full app is there when you want your library and streaks.

  • Ghost alignment overlay

    Yesterday’s photo fades over the viewfinder with adjustable opacity so pose, angle, and framing stay consistent day after day.

  • Time-lapse export

    Turn your series into H.264 or HEVC video. Pick frame rate and resolution, then save or share.

  • On-device eye alignment

    Optional Vision analysis keeps your face centered across frames. Processing stays on your Mac.

  • Library & streaks

    Browse by month, track your current and longest streak, and open any day full-screen.

  • Privacy mode

    Hide in-app thumbnails with placeholders. Your photos stay untouched in the Photos app.

How it works

Three steps to your time-lapse

  1. 01

    Capture

    Open PocketPic, line up with the ghost overlay, and save one photo to your album.

  2. 02

    Build the streak

    Come back each day. Review the library, prune frames, and watch the streak grow.

  3. 03

    Export

    Generate a montage with optional eye alignment and share your transformation.
