Features

Built with simplicity in mind

Everything you need to start your daily photo project. Capture, organize, and create time-lapses of your transformation.

1

Capture daily

Access from the menu bar for quick captures. Open the full app to browse your library and manage streaks.

2

Alignment overlay

Your previous photo ghosts over the viewfinder with adjustable opacity for consistent framing.

3

Export video

Turn your series into H.264 or HEVC video. Choose frame rate and resolution. Save or share.

4

Face alignment

Optional Vision-based analysis keeps your face centered and consistent across all frames.

5

Photo library

Browse by month. Track streaks. Full-screen detail view. All organized in one place.

6

Privacy first

Hide thumbnails in the app. Your photos stay in Photos app, completely untouched.

How it works

Three simple steps

1. Capture

Open the app or menu bar. Line up with yesterday's overlay and save your photo.

2. Organize

Review your library. Track streaks. Manage your photos as your project grows.

3. Export

Generate a time-lapse video with optional face alignment. Share your transformation.

Built for macOS

Native. Fast. Private.

SwiftUI app using AVFoundation, PhotoKit, and Vision—all on-device processing, no cloud sync.
