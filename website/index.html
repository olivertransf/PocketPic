Features

Everything you need for a daily photo project

PocketPic keeps the workflow simple: shoot, review, and export. No accounts, no cloud uploads — your photos stay in your library.

📷

Daily capture

Take one photo per day with a full-screen camera. On Mac, snap a quick shot from the menu bar without opening the full app.

👻

Alignment overlay

Your previous photo ghosts over the viewfinder with adjustable opacity so you can match framing, pose, and angle every day.

🎬

Time-lapse export

Turn your photo series into an H.264 or HEVC montage video. Choose frame rate (5–60 fps), resolution, and save to Photos, Files, or Share.

👁

Eye alignment

Optional on-device Vision analysis aligns position, rotation, and scale across frames so your face stays centered in the final video.

🖼

Photo library

Browse photos in a month-grouped mosaic. Track your current and longest streak. Zoom into full-screen detail view.

🔒

Privacy mode

Hide thumbnails in the in-app gallery with neutral placeholders — your photos stay in the Photos app, untouched.

How it works

Three steps to your time-lapse

Capture daily

Open the camera, line up with yesterday's overlay, and save one photo to your PocketPic album.

Build your streak

Review your library, track streaks, and select or delete photos as your project grows.

Export video

Generate a montage with optional eye alignment. Share your transformation anywhere.

Platforms

Native on Apple devices

SwiftUI app built with AVFoundation for camera and export, PhotoKit for library sync, and Vision for on-device face alignment.
